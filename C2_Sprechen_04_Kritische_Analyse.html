Kritische Analyse
Analysing texts, positions and arguments at C2

Task: Critically analyse a text or statement

Read this claim: 'Kuenstliche Intelligenz wird innerhalb von 20 Jahren alle menschliche Arbeit ersetzen.' Analyse it critically: What does the claim assume? What evidence would be needed? What does it overlook? What rhetorical function does it serve? Present your analysis in 3 minutes.

💡 C2 tip: Critical analysis means separating what is claimed, what is implied, what is evidenced, and what rhetorical function the statement serves.
💬 Useful phrases for this task

🎯 C2 Coaching Tips
Unpacking assumptions: Welche Praemissen stecken hinter dieser These? Sind sie begruendet?
Rhetorical function: Was will diese Aussage beim Hoerer bewirken? Angst? Begeisterung?
Distinguishing: Zwischen totaler Automatisierung und partieller Unterstuetzung muss man klar unterscheiden.
