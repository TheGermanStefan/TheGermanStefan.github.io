📘 Sprachphilosophie
At the highest level you engage with Sprachphilosophie: how language shapes thought, how meaning is constructed, and the limits of linguistic expression.
ConceptThinkerCore idea
SprachrelativismusSapir-WhorfLanguage shapes perception and thought
SprechakttheorieAustin / SearleUtterances are actions: promises, commands
SprachspieleWittgensteinMeaning comes from use in social practices
HermeneutikGadamerUnderstanding requires interpreting within tradition
DekonstruktionDerridaMeaning is unstable and deferred
Metapher als ErkenntnisLakoff / JohnsonWe think in metaphors: Argument is war
💡 Examples
  • Die Grenzen meiner Sprache bedeuten die Grenzen meiner Welt. (Wittgenstein)
    Language limits what can be thought.
  • Ein Versprechen geben is a speech act - it changes social reality.
    Austin: saying = doing.
  • Argument is war - we ATTACK positions, DEFEND views. (Lakoff & Johnson)
    Conceptual metaphors structure thought.
