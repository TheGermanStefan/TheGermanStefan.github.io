💡 Quick Tip: The golden rule: the verb is ALWAYS in position 2 in a German main clause. If you start with an adverb or time expression, the subject moves to position 3 — but the verb stays at position 2!

✅ Richtig oder Falsch?
Are the statements correct (R) or incorrect (F)?
#StatementR / F
1.In German, the verb always goes in the second position in main clauses.
2.In a Yes/No question, the subject comes first and the verb second.
3.„Heute ich gehe ins Kino" is correct German word order.
4.In W-questions (Who? Where? When?), the verb is in the second position after the question word.
5.When a sentence starts with an adverb, the subject moves to position 3 (after the verb).
