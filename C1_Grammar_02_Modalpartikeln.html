📘 Modalpartikeln
Modalpartikeln (modal particles) are untranslatable nuance words that express the speaker's attitude, assumptions, and relationship with the listener. They are essential for natural, idiomatic German at C1 level.

They never carry sentence stress and cannot be translated word-for-word.
ParticleCore meaningExample
dochassumption/contradictionDas weißt du doch! — You know that (surely)!
jashared knowledge/mild warningPass ja auf! — Do be careful!
malsoftening, casualnessKomm mal her. — Come here (a sec).
wohlprobability/hedgingEr ist wohl krank. — He's probably ill.
eigentlichactually/by the wayWas machst du eigentlich? — What do you actually do?
halt/ebeninevitability (regional)Das ist halt so. — That's just how it is.
💡 Examples
  • Das hast du doch gewusst! — You knew that all along!
    doch = contradicting the implicit claim that they didn't know
  • Lies mal den Bericht. — Have a read of the report.
    mal = makes the request less direct, more casual
  • Sie ist wohl noch unterwegs. — She's probably still on her way.
    wohl = expressing probability without certainty
