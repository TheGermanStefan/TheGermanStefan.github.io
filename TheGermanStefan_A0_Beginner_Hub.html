⭐ Special German Characters

German has 4 extra characters not found in English. These are unique to German!

Ä / ä
"ay" sound (like "air")
Ärger (anger)
Ö / ö
"ur" sound (round lips)
Öl (oil)
Ü / ü
"ee" + round lips
über (over)
ß
double "ss" sound
Straße (street)
🎯 Quick Pronunciation Rules
ei = sounds like "eye" → nein (no)
ie = sounds like "ee" → viel (much)
eu = sounds like "oy" → neu (new)
au = sounds like "ow" → Haus (house)
ch = soft "h" or "k" → ich (I)
w = sounds like "v" → wie (how)

Erste Sätze – First Sentences
Build your first sentences in German. Introduce yourself!
🧩 Key Verbs First
sein — to be
ich bin — I am
du bist — you are
er/sie/es ist — he/she/it is
wir sind — we are
heißen — to be called
ich heiße — my name is
du heißt — your name is
er/sie heißt — his/her name is
wir heißen — we are called
kommen — to come from
ich komme aus — I come from
du kommst aus — you come from
er/sie kommt aus — he/she comes from
wohnen — to live
ich wohne in — I live in
du wohnst in — you live in
er/sie wohnt in — he/she lives in
✏️ Build Your Introduction

Fill in the blanks to create your personal introduction in German. Use your real information!

1. My name is…

e.g. Ich heiße Maria
2. I am … years old
Ich binJahre alt.
e.g. Ich bin 28 Jahre alt.
3. I come from…
