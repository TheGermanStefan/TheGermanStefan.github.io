✅ Richtig oder Falsch?
Are the statements correct (R) or incorrect (F)?
#StatementR / F
1.„Sie" (with capital S) is always the formal form of "you".
2.„ihr" is used when talking about one person informally.
3.„es" is used for neuter nouns (das-nouns).
4.„wir" includes the speaker and at least one other person.
5.„sie" (lowercase) can mean both "she" AND "they" in German.
