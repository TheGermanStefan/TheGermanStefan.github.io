✅ Richtig oder Falsch?
Are the statements correct (R) or incorrect (F)?
#StatementR / F
1. „Sie" can refer to both a woman (she) and formal address (you).
2. „Ihr" means "he" in German.
3. „Wir" is used when speaking about a group that includes the speaker.
