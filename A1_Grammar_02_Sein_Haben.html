✅ Richtig oder Falsch?
Are the statements correct (R) or incorrect (F)?
#StatementR / F
1.„Ich bin Hunger" is correct German for "I am hungry".
2.„Er hat 30 Jahre alt" is how you say "He is 30 years old" in German.
3.The form „sind" is used for both „wir" and „sie/Sie".
4.„Ich habe Durst" means "I am thirsty" (literally: "I have thirst").
5.„Bist du müde?" is the correct way to ask "Are you tired?" informally.
