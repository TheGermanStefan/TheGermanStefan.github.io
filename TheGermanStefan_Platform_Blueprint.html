🎯 Mission & Positioning

What we are

The world's most exam-focused, community-driven German learning platform. Not another grammar website — a results-driven membership for people who need to pass a specific Goethe exam and want a live, supportive community around them while they do it.


Why we win

  • 30 full Goethe mock exams (A1–C2) — more than any free resource
  • Exercises mapped to exact Goethe module structure
  • A live human face behind it: Stefan, TikTok/IG creator
  • Weekly Stammtisch — you practice with real people
  • Priced for the world, not just Europe

Who we serve

Primary: Adults worldwide preparing for a specific Goethe exam for visa, university, or job reasons. High motivation, time pressure, willing to pay.
Secondary: German learners wanting structured self-study with community accountability. No exam deadline but want progress.
Tertiary: Teachers and tutors who assign exercises and mock exams to their own students.
Channel: All reached primarily through TheGermanStefan on TikTok, Instagram, Facebook, YouTube.

Hosting the HTML files

Option A (Easiest): Upload HTML files directly to Skool as downloadable resources per module.
Option B (Best UX): Host files on a simple website (e.g. Netlify — free) and embed/link from Skool. Members open in browser, fully interactive.
Option C (Full control): Custom WordPress site for the exercises, Skool for community. Login integrates via email. More work but most scalable.
